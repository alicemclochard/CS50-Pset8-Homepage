shortbread cookies

ingredients

  • 2 cups butter, softened
  • 4 to 4-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 325. cream butter and brown sugar. add flour. knead dough.
  2. roll out dough. cut into shape. prick with fork. refrigerate.
  3. place cookies on baking sheet. bake 20-25 mins.

notes

  • cut shortbread while still warm for even edges
